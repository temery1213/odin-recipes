Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Heavy whipping cream

Directions

  1. Boil water
  2. Add nooddles to boiling water
  3. Grate cheese
  4. Let noodles reach al dente, then strain
  5. Return noodles to pot and add a bit of heavy cream
  6. Add cheese
  7. Stir until thoroughly mixed
  8. Enjoy!
