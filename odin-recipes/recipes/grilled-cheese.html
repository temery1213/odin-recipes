Grilled Cheese

Classic Comfort

Grilled cheese is a simple yet classic dish that is full of comfort.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Spread butter to one side of two slices of bread
  2. Add olive oil to pan
  3. Heat pan to med-high
  4. Lay one slice of bread with butter side facing down
  5. Add cheese
  6. Place second piece of bread on top with butter side up
  7. Cook till golden
  8. Flip
  9. Cook till golden
  10. Serve
